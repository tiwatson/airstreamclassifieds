<p>The water system has had a custom on/off valve installed in the small cabinet beneath the stove that physically prevents the water reaching the water pump. Why? After replacing three water pumps in the first year of ownership due to the back-flow preventer failing in the water pump we decided to fix the problem permanently. When connected to city water the water pressure from the city water system "pushes" water back into the water pump. When the water pump back-flow preventer which is intended to stop this fails, the water goes through the pump and into the freshwater tank, usually overfilling it before you are aware of the problem. Our tank overflow always expelled the excess water outside of the trailer but this was just a waste of water and made a mess of many camp sites. Since we made this modification we have had no problems.</p><br />
